FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a coding background to use Transacbo?
No. Transacbo comes with a desktop app and setup wizard. You just enter your settings — no Python, no terminal, no coding required.
Do I need an Interactive Brokers account?
Yes. Transacbo connects to IBKR via their official API (TWS/Gateway). IBKR accounts are free to open. You can start with a paper trading account at zero cost.
How much capital do I need?
IBKR requires a $10,000 minimum for margin accounts. However, Transacbo works with any capital size — you set the risk per trade in the settings.
Does the bot trade 24/7?
Transacbo trades during regular market hours for your selected market. It scans every 15 minutes during market hours and exits positions before close if configured.
Is my IBKR account safe?
Yes. Transacbo runs entirely on your own computer — your credentials never leave your machine. We have zero access to your account.
Can I run it on paper trading first?
Absolutely. Transacbo has a paper trading mode built in. We strongly recommend running it on paper for at least 2-4 weeks before going live.
What if I want to cancel?
Cancel anytime from your Whop dashboard — no questions asked, no penalty. Your subscription ends at the next billing date.
